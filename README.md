# The Spatial Studio

A portfolio of spatial research, curating works in **physical geography**, **human geography**, and **technical geography**.

This repository serves a dual purpose: it showcases completed projects while also functioning as a living workspace for ongoing research and experimentation.

---

## About

The Spatial Studio brings together spatial analysis, cartography, and geographic research across the three core branches of the discipline. Projects span finished, portfolio-ready work as well as in-progress investigations, methodological notes, and exploratory analyses.

---

## Research Areas

### Physical Geography
Process-based studies of the Earth's surface; geomorphology, hydrology, climatology, and the systems that shape the physical landscape.

### Human Geography
Spatial dimensions of human activity ; settlement, mobility, economic and social patterns, and the relationships between people and place. It is also spanned to urban & regional planning project which is very geography at its core.

### Technical Geography
The methods and tools behind spatial inquiry; GIS, remote sensing, spatial statistics, geocomputation, and data visualization. Every projects in this portfolio use this technical principles.

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **GIS & Mapping:** ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, GRASS, SAGA, Whitebox, HEC-RAS, SWAT
- **Programming & Analysis:** Python (GeoPandas, Rasterio, Shapely, GRASS)
- **Remote Sensing:** ArcGIS Pro, GRASS, Google Earth Engine
